2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

DryFire is a mobile application designed to assist with dry fire training. The app provides multiple tools for training and documentation to include:
- Timer functions with countdown and stopwatch capabilities
- Pre-built and custom training drills
- Score calculators (USPSA, Top Gun)
- Training logs and session tracking
- Metronome and interval training tools
- Par time training features
- Audio cues (beeps and voice commands)
